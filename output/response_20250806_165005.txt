Question:
Tell me about the company.

Answer:
Standard Chartered is a bank that operates with a strong focus on commerce and prosperity, driven by its unique diversity. The company's purpose is to facilitate commerce in the real economy, while also contributing to creating healthier and happier communities. Standard Chartered has identified three key strategic pillars, including business, community, government, and clients, which are supported by three enablers: People & Culture, Ways of Working, and Innovation & Technology. The company is committed to investing in its people, digital capabilities, and infrastructure to enhance its clients' banking experience, while also prioritizing sustainability and control.

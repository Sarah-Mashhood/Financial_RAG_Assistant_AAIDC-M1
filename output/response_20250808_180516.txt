Question:
Compare revenue and net profit year-over-year.

Answer:
Based on the provided financial report, the revenue for the three months period ended 31 March 2025 (Un-audited) is PKR 23,101 million, while the revenue for the same period last year (31 March 2024) is PKR 29,455 million. This represents a year-over-year decrease of PKR 6,354 million, or 21.7%.

Question:
What was the efficiency ratio in the last quarter?

Answer:
Based on the provided financial report, the operating expenses in the last quarter (31 March 2025) were PKR 5,437 million, and the revenue was PKR 23,101 million.

To calculate the efficiency ratio, we can use the following formula:

Efficiency Ratio = Operating Expenses / Revenue

Efficiency Ratio = PKR 5,437 million / PKR 23,101 million
= 0.236 or 23.6%

Therefore, the efficiency ratio in the last quarter was 23.6%.
